Our Story

AZ Fancy Home Collection is a local company that believes in improving everyday life by offering a wide range of home design items.
AZ is for A to Z, which means you can get everything you need to make your home more beautiful than it already is in this shop.
This home décor firm was founded because of the owner's passion for house design, and she hopes to share her knowledge with other homeowners so that they can reach their ideal fancy home as well.
All the things it sells are of exceptional quality and were hand-picked by the proprietor to ensure that they were of high quality. Homeowners will not be sorry they chose this shop to be a part of their magnificent home, and they will undoubtedly feel at ease and satisfied with the results.

The Founder/CEO

The Founder and CEO of AZ Fancy Home Collection is Ms. Ulyzza Gimenez Peñalosa. She was born on October 21, 2000. She started online business in year 2022 and after three years of hard work she now achieved her dream of establishing her own home décor brand. She graduated with a bachelor’s degree in Accounting Information System and is currently studying to pursue one of her dreams of becoming a Certified Public Accountant. She loves nature and one of her hobbies is travelling and adventure. She also enjoys designing and decorating her home. Her biggest dream is to make her home décor brand be known internationally and travel around the world with her family.
